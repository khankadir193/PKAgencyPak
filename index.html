HOW TO PLAY

  • Top 4 Agencies have to register their ids with SK employees to participate in this event,
  • Each agencies will have 3 talents in it.
  • The talent from these agencies will compete in a 3 Vs 3 PK battle.
  • A Host must invite opponent host first then need to invite 2 other talents from their agency to compete with opponent agency.
  • Top 4 agency that earns maximum gems will go to the ﬁnal round.
  • The talent that wins maximum gems from the qualiﬁed agency will go to the ﬁnal round.
  • The winner of this tournament will be decided based on the gems they receive in the Final Round.
  • Talents will be ranked on the leader board according to the number of gems they receive in each Round.
  • Users will be ranked according to their spending on each round of the tournament.
  • The maximum spender will be the winner of the tournament.
